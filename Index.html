Common Questions

How long does construction take?
Our average build time for the Outdoor Living Package is 45-60 days from the day of excavation. Permitting times vary by city, but we handle the entire process for you to ensure it moves as quickly as possible.
What's included in the package price?
The package includes 3D design, structural engineering, excavation, steel, plumbing, electrical, shotcrete, premium Pebble Tec finish, waterline tile, Hayward smart equipment pad, LED lighting, and options for decking and a fire feature or BBQ island.
Do you handle permits?
Yes, we handle 100% of the permitting process, HOA approvals (if required), and engineering plans. You won't have to deal with city bureaucracy.
What financing options are available?
We partner with several top-tier lenders specializing in home improvement and pool loans (like Lyon Financial and LightStream). We can often secure approvals within 24 hours with zero down and terms up to 20 years to keep payments low.
How long is the warranty?
We provide a Lifetime Structural Warranty on the pool shell. Equipment (pumps, heaters) comes with a standard 3-year manufacturer warranty, and finishes/plaster are covered for 5-10 years depending on the exact material chosen.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Riverside County, focusing heavily on Murrieta, Temecula, Menifee, Wildomar, Lake Elsinore, and Winchester.
